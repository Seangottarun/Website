Things that I've done

National University of Singapore (NUS) Student Researcher

Singapore

May-Aug 2019

This past summer, I had the privilege of working at the National University of Singapore. Over the past 12 weeks, I worked with Professor Raisul Islam to research indirect evaporative coolers (IECs). My research on IECs mainly consisted of conducting a literature review, developing a simplified mathematical model of a counter-flow M-cycle IEC, and performing numerical simulations in MATLAB. I used my model and simulations to determine ideal design parameters and configurations for the potential usage of IECs in Singapore. Finally, my work culminated in a final report where I discussed my model and detailed my findings.
